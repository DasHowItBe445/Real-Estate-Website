Overview

Oberoi Garden City Thane is an integrated development nestled in the lap of nature offering extensive space to recreate and engage. Spread across 75 acres, Oberoi Garden City Thane located on Pokhran Road No 2 is set to establish new standards in luxury living offering homes. The first phase of the development will consist of 5 Residential Towers with over 30 world-class amenities, a 5-star deluxe JW Marriott Hotel Thane Garden City, an exclusive Private Membership Club and an Oberoi International School at Pokhran. It is a place where holistic living takes a new meaning – where communities interact, engage, and thrive – all in the heart of nature.
